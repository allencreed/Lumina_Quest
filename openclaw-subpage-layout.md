## 📝 Article Body Format

### Introduction Section
- Short, impactful opening paragraph
- Hook: Address pain point (missed calls, lost leads, overwhelm)
- Credibility: Position as Atlanta AI expert
- Clear value proposition

### Reason #1 Section
**Heading:** 24/7 Customer Response Without the Staffing Costs

**Content:** 2-3 paragraphs explaining the problem/solution
- Real stats/examples
- Benefits bullet list

**Visual:** Icon or illustration of clock + AI brain

### Reason #2 Section  
**Heading:** Seamless Integration With Your Existing Tools

**Content:** 2-3 paragraphs
- Address integration concerns
- List compatible tools

**Visual:** Connected app icons ( Slack, Gmail, Calendar, etc.)

### Reason #3 Section
**Heading:** Built for Results, Not Just Hype

**Content:** 2-3 paragraphs
- Differentiation from competitors
- Testimonial or case study reference

**Visual:** Growth chart or checkmarks
